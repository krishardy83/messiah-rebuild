Welcome to Web Services

The Web Services Office at Messiah University, housed within the Office of Marketing and Communications, functions as the central hub overseeing the University's external website and associated web services. The office operates under the leadership of the Director of Web and Digital Marketing, with day-to-day management entrusted to the Web Services Manager.

Our basic web design and consulting services are generally provided at no cost to campus clients. However, should the need arise for services beyond our internal capabilities, collaboration with an external vendor may be required, incurring additional expenses.

Need web help?

The Web Services Team is responsible for overseeing the management of our helpdesk ticket program.

If you encounter any issues on the Messiah University website, or if you need assistance with your website, please submit a helpdesk ticket by clicking the button below and we will address your concerns at the earliest opportunity.

We are readily available and happy to assist you!
